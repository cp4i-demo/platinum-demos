### 1 - Provision your demo environment

The Automation Decision Services (ADS) demo is simply based on the Telco retention sample provided in the default ADS install. <br/>
You must install CP4BA 21.0.2 or provision ROKS environment that will be available for 2 weeks at no charge. <br/>
This document explains how to get a free pre-configured Cloud Pak for Business Automation environment:

<details>

<summary>Reserve your CP4BA preconfigured environment on ROKS</summary>
<br/>

1. Log into the <a href="http://techzone.ibm.com" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">IBM Technology Zone</a> using your IBM ID. Then, click on the **Activation Kits** tab. <br/><br/>

![TechZone](./images/1-Techzone_600.png)
<br/><br/>

2. Scroll down to click on the **Cloud Pak for Business Automation** tile. <br/><br/>

![CP4BA Activation Kit](./images/1-CP4BA_Activation_Kit_600.png)
<br/><br/>

3. Click on the **Environments - Reserve Here!** tab. Then, select the **Reserve Here! - Cloud Pak for Business Automation OpenShift Demo Environments** tile. <br/><br/>

4. Click on the **Preinstalled OpenShift Environments** tab. Then, select the latest version of the **Preinstalled CP4BA on Red Hat OpenShift on IBM Cloud**. <br/><br/>

5. Select **Reserve now**. Then, after entering the **Purpose**, **Preferred Geography**, and **Notes**, click on **Submit**. <br/><br/>

6. When you are done, you will see a confirmation message. In addition, you will receive a confirmation email with all the access links. <br/><br/>

![Confirmation](./images/1-Confirmation_600.png)
<br/><br/>

![Confirmation Email](./images/1-Confirmation_email_600.png)
<br/><br/>

7. Then, you will receive an email confirming that your instance is provisioned. It may take 30 to 45 minutes. <br/><br/>

![Instance Provisioned](./images/1-Ready_email_600.png)
<br/><br/>

**[Go to top](#top)**

</details>

<details>

<summary>Access your provisioned CP4BA environment</summary>
<br/>

1. Log into the <a href="http://techzone.ibm.com" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">IBM Technology Zone</a>. <br/><br/>

2. At the upper-right corner, click on the **Profile** icon. Then, click on **Reservations**. You will then be directed to the **My reservations** page, in which you can see your reservation details. Note: Bookmark this page for ease of reference. <br/><br/>

![Reservations](./images/2-Reservations_600.png)
<br/><br/>

![My Reservations](./images/2-My_Reservations_600.png)
<br/><br/>

3. Once provisioned, your instance should look like the screenshot below. <br/><br/>

![Provisioned Instance](./images/2-Provisioned_Instance_600.png)
<br/><br/>

**[Go to top](#top)**

</details>

<details>

<summary>Access your OpenShift instance</summary>
<br/>

When your instance is provisioned, you should receive a confirmation email with the access links. If the links are not attached to the email, please refer to the steps below. <br/><br/>

1. Go to the <a href="https://github.ibm.com/jgoodhue/cp4ba-roks-install" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">START HERE</a> section in your OpenShift Instance. Note: The link is also provided in the confirmation email. <br/><br/>

![IBM Technology Zone](./images/3-Start_Here_600.png)
<br/><br/>

2. Click to open the **README.md** file, and scroll down to click on **Preinstalled Cluster**. Under **Instructions**, click on **2 - Wait for your cluster** to expand the section and follow step 2.2. <br/><br/>

3. From your **Reservation** page, double-click on your instance, and copy your **Environment ID**. <br/><br/>

![Environment ID](./images/3-EnvironmentID_600.png)
<br/><br/>

4. Log into your <a href="https://cloud.ibm.com/kubernetes/clusters?platformType=openshift" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">IBM Cloud</a> account. <br/><br/>

5. On the top menu, swap to the **DTESQUAD** account. <br/><br/>

![DTESQUAD](./images/3-DTE_Squad_600.png)
<br/><br/>

6. From the **Clusters** menu, search for your cluster using the **Environment ID** that was copied from step 3. <br/><br/>

![Clusters](./images/3-Cluster_Menu_600.png)
<br/><br/>

7. Check that your cluster's state is green, and double-click on it. Check that your **Ingress subdomain** is set up, and open your **OpenShift web console**. <br/><br/>

8. Open the **Workloads** menu, and select **Config Maps**. Then, set your project to **dtecp4ba**. <br/> <br/>

9. Search for **access** to access the **icp4Adeploy-cp4ba-access-info** file. Open this file to access all your access links and credentials to the various environments. Note: You have the **ADS-runtime-access-info** in that file as well. <br/> <br/>

![Access](./images/3-Access_600.png)
<br/><br/>

10. The important link ie the **BAStudio URL**. From this URL, you will be able to access the key elements of the demo: <br/>
•	Business Automation Studio <br/>
•	Application Designer <br/>
•	Automation Decision Services <br/> <br/>

11. You are now ready to set up the demo. Open **BAStudio** from the ADS link in the access file.

<br/><br/>

**[Go to top](#top)**

</details>
